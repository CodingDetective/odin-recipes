Mango Smoothie

Description

This mango smoothie is sure to cool you down on a hot day. Perfect for an after-dinner dessert!

Ingredients

  • 2 fresh mangoes or 2 cups of frozen mango
  • 1 small frozen banana (or half of a large banana), you can use unfrozen if using frozen mango
  • 1/2 cup milk, dairy or dairy-free
  • 1/2 cup yogurt, dairy or dairy-free

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a high-powered blender and blend until creamy.
  2. Enjoy!
