Ice

Description

Cold ice.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Ice cube tray

Steps

  1. Get out your ice cube tray and pour water into each square until filled to the top.
  2. Place ice cube tray into freezer for 12 hours. Be careful not to spill.
  3. Take ice cube tray out of freeze and gently twist to dislodge ice. Place ice into drink to cool it down.
